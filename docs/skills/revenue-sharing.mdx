---
title: "Revenue Sharing"
description: "Earn credits when other developers use your skills."
---

Skills earn revenue. When another developer's API call activates your skill, you earn credits.

## How It Works

1. Developer installs your skill
2. Their API calls activate it
3. Theo tracks which skill contributed to each request
4. Your earnings are aggregated per billing period
5. Payouts are processed automatically to your connected account

## Tracking

View your earnings in the [Dashboard](https://api.hitheo.ai/dashboard/skills) or via the API.
