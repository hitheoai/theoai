Manage your account, organization, and preferences from the Settings page.

## Account Settings

- **Profile** — Update your display name and email address
- **Email preferences** — Configure notification preferences (usage alerts, billing, skill updates)
- **API keys** — Quick link to the [API Keys](/dashboard/api-keys) management page
- **Security** — Enable MFA, manage active sessions, view login history

## Organization Settings

If you belong to an organization:

- **Team members** — Invite members, assign roles (admin, member, viewer)
- **Billing** — Org-level credit pool and spending caps
- **Connectors** — Configure external system integrations (CRMs, APIs, data sources)
- **Webhooks** — Set up webhook URLs for event notifications (completions, skill installs, etc.)
- **SSO** — Configure single sign-on for your organization

## Danger Zone

- **Delete account** — Permanently delete your account and all associated data. Contact support to initiate.
